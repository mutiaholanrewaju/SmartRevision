2. Voici (This is / Here is)

It is used to showcase any noun close to us.

Examples:

  1. Voici le cahier (This is the book)
  2. Voici le lion (This is the lion)
  3. Voici le bébé (This is the baby)

3. Voilà (That is / There is)

It is used to present a person or an object that is far from you.

Examples:

  1. Voilà monsieur Taiwo (That is Mr Taiwo)
  2. Voilà le dindon (That is the turkey)
