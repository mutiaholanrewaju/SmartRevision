1. Air

Air is a gaseous substance we cannot see but we can feel it. It can occupy space.

Existence of Air:

  1. Air from the mouth into the balloon
  2. When balloon bursts, air in it escape
  3. Strong air bend the tree
  4. Air blow and dry cloth
  5. Wind blow sand and paper to the sky

2. Soil

Soil is the top part of the earth on which plant grow.

Types of soil:

  1. Sandy soil
  2. Clay soil
  3. Loamy soil

Uses of soil:

  1. We plant on the soil
  2. Some animals lives in the soil
  3. Plant grow from the soil
  4. Water is gotten from the soil

Component of soil:

  1. Water
  2. Animal in the soil
  3. Soil air
  4. Rock particles e.g stone
  5. Organic particles

3. Light Energy

Light is a type of energy that help us to see things around.

Sources of Light:

  1. Sun
  2. Star
  3. Candle
  4. Fireflies
  1. Electric bulb
  2. Lamp
  3. Lighter

Uses of light:

  1. It is used to operate machine
  2. Sunlight makes plant to grow
  3. It helps to dry our clothes
  4. It helps us to see in the dark

4. Colour identification of objects

Colour is how an object looks when light shines on it.

Natural colour of objects:

  1. Cotton - white
  2. Tomatoes - red
  3. Charcoal - black
  4. Sky - blue

Types of Colour:

  1. Primary colours: This consist of red, yellow and blue
  2. Secondary colours: This consist of green, purple and orange

Uses of Colour:

  1. It is used as decoration
  2. It is used to beautify an object
  3. It is used in traffic light
  4. It is used to identify logo of companies
