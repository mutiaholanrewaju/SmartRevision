📖 Revision Notes

1. Rules and Regulation

Rules and regulation are things we must do and not to do.

Types of Rules and Regulation:

  1. Rules and regulation at school
  2. Rules and regulation at home
  3. Rules and regulation at office
  4. Rules and regulation in the worship places

2. Benefits of obeying rules

  1. Love
  2. Gifts
  3. Reward
  4. Praise
  5. Scholarship

3. Disobeying rules and regulation

Disobedience means going against the rules and regulations. When people disobey, they face results.

Results of Disobeying:

  1. Punishment at home
  2. Punishment at school
  3. Loss of freedom
  4. Loss of Friends
  5. Discrimination

4. Importance of School Rules

  1. To be orderliness
  2. To keep pupils in check
  3. To have a conducive environment
  4. To respect the school authority
  5. To maintain peace and unity
  6. To set a standard in our society
  7. To provide safety and security
  8. To provide accountability in our school
  9. To respect the teachers

5. Government Rules

Results of Obeying:

  1. It keeps everyone safe
  2. It makes everyone feels comfortable
  3. It creates a better society
  4. It helps to maintain safety
  5. It prepare someone to live in a bigger society

Results of Disobeying Government:

  1. Fines
  2. Arrest
  3. Jail terms
  4. Imprisonment
  5. Loss of rights
  6. Legal action

6. Obe-di-en-ce

Obedience means doing what you are told to do.

People we must obey:

  1. Parents
  2. Teachers
  3. Elderly ones
  4. Guardians

Ways to show obedience:

  1. Listening to your parents, teachers or elderly ones
  2. Helping your parents
  3. Following the rules given

Importance of obedience:

  1. It builds trust
  2. It brings blessing and rewards
  3. It keeps us safe from danger
  4. It makes our parents and teachers happy
