# SmartRevision Hub 📚✨

**SmartRevision** is a premium, interactive learning platform designed specifically for Primary 1 students (following the Lagos State Unified Scheme). It transforms traditional holiday revision into an engaging, digital experience that helps children master their second-term notes through study guides, gamified spelling practice, and realistic mock examinations.

## 🚀 Live Demo
Access the official app here: https://smart-revision-iota.vercel.app/

## 🌟 Key Features

### 1. 🐝 Spelling Bee Mastery 
Our flagship tool designed to improve orthography and visual memory.
*   **90+ Curriculum Words:** Carefully selected from Science, ICT, Social Studies, and Arabic notes.
*   **Look-Copy-Cover-Write:** A scientifically proven 4-step method for learning complex spellings.
*   **Teacher-Paced Audio:** Crystal-clear enunciation at a controlled speed (0.65x) to help children hear every phoneme.
*   **Gamified Progress:** Children earn stars and unlock levels as they progress.

### 2. 📖 Integrated "Study-First" Notes
Every subject mock exam features a dual-tab system:
*   **Study Tab:** Concise, numbered notes organized in bordered boxes for high visibility.
*   **Anti-Cheat Logic:** The notes tab automatically locks the moment a child begins the exam, ensuring the test results are an honest reflection of their knowledge.

### 3. 📝 Interactive Mock Exams
Self-grading exams mimicking real classroom standards:
*   **Basic Science:** Air, Soil, Light Energy, and Colours.
*   **Arabic Language:** Letters, similar sounds, stationery, and action sentences.
*   **Islamic Studies (IRS):** Surahs, Taharah, Wudu, and Obedience.
*   **Civic Education:** Rules, Regulation, Heroes, and Handcrafts.
*   **P.H.E & French:** Vocabulary, sports skills, and numbers.

### 4. 📱 Progressive Web App (PWA) Experience
*   **Mobile Optimized:** Large fonts and touch-friendly buttons designed for small fingers.
*   **Offline Ready:** Can be added to the phone's home screen to work like a native app.

## 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Parent's Guide: How to "Install"
To use this like an app on your phone:
1.  Open the link in **Safari** (iPhone) or **Chrome** (Android).
2.  Tap the **Share** button (iPhone) or the **three dots** (Android).
3.  Select **'Add to Home Screen'**.
4.  The SmartRevision icon will now appear on your phone's dashboard!

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
*   **HTML5/CSS3:** Structure and responsive design.
*   **Tailwind CSS:** Modern UI styling.
*   **JavaScript (ES6):** Interactive logic, grading engines, and state management.
*   **Web Speech API:** Native browser voice synthesis for the Spelling Bee.
*   **Local Storage API:** To save the child's stars and progress locally on the device.
