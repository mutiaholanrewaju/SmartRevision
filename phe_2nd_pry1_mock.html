Local Game (In-di-ge-nous Game)

Local games are simple games that can be played by everyone in the environment.

Examples of Local Game:

  1. Rats and Rabbits
  2. Cat and rat
  3. Fire on the mountain
  4. Ten-ten
  5. Boju-boju (Hide and seeks)
  6. Ayo

Football game

Football is a game played on the field.

It is played by eleven (11) players.

The person that coordinates the game is called referee.

Skills in football games are:

  1. Kicking
  2. Stopping
  3. Passing
  4. Dribbling
  5. Heading
  6. Throwing

Safety rules in football game

  1. Warm up well
  2. Do not push while playing
  3. Take a break when tired
  4. Wear the right sport outfit
  5. Do not play on hard surface
  6. Obey the officials of the game e.g. referee
  7. Inform someone if you feel sick or tired
  8. Do not play on wet or slippery pitch

Swimming

Swimming is the movement of using arms and legs to float inside the water.

Swimming skills for beginners:

  1. Coming in and out of the water
  2. Adjusting the water
  3. Peace down when floating
  4. Never swim alone
  5. Breathing

Types of swimming:

  1. Free style swimming
  2. Front crawl swimming
  3. Back strokes
  4. Butterfly strokes
  5. Breast strokes
  6. Side strokes

Safety Rules in Swimming

  1. Never swim alone
  2. Always wear a life vest
  3. Be careful when getting in and out of the pool
  4. Play carefully
  5. Stay within your ability
