2. Suratul Nas (Quran 114)

The Verses:

  1. Qul audhu birabi-n-Nas
  2. Maliki-n-Nas
  3. ILLahi-n-Nas
  4. Min sharri-l-waswasil-khanas
  5. Aladhi yuwas wisu-fi-suduri-n-nas
  6. Minal jinnat wa Nas

Translation / Meaning:

  1. Say, I seek refuge with the lord of mankind
  2. The king of mankind
  3. The God of mankind
  4. From the mischief of the whisper of evil
  5. Who whisper into the heart of man
  6. From among the Jinn and Men

Lessons from the surah:

  1. We ask Allah to protect us
  2. Allah is the king who controls everyone
  3. Allah is the king who controls everything
  4. Allah is the only God we worship

When to recite:

  1. When praying
  2. When travelling
  3. When in danger or afraid

3. At-Taharah (Cleanliness)

  1. Cleanliness is part of the teachings of Islam.
  2. A Muslim must always be clean.
  3. His clothes, house, class, mosque and compound must be clean.

Importance of Cleanliness

  1. Allah is clean, one must be clean to worship him.
  2. Cleanliness teaches one to have good health.
  3. Cleanliness removes bad smells from the body and surroundings.
  4. A Muslim must take his bath everyday and brush teeth.

4. Suratul Falaq (Quran 113)

The Verses:

  1. Qul Audhu birabbi-l-falaq
  2. Min sharri ma khalaq
  3. Wa min sharr ghasiquin idha waqab
  4. Wa min sharri-n-fathatin filhuqad
  5. Wa min sharri hasidin idha hasad

Meaning of Suratul Falaq:

  1. Say, I seek refuge from the lord of the dawn
  2. From the mischief of what he created
  3. And from the mischief of darkness it envelops
  4. And from the mischief of those who practice secret acts
  5. And from the mischief of the envious one as he practices envy

When to recite:

  1. During Salat
  2. When in Danger or Afraid

5. Steps of Al-Wudu

Al-wudu is done by washing parts that are usually exposed.

  1. Wash the hands
  2. Rinse the mouth
  3. Clean the nose
  4. Wash the face
  5. Wash the arms up to the elbows
  6. Wipe the head and ears
  7. Wash the feet

6. Obedience to Allah & Parents

Obedience means doing what you are told.

How to Obey Allah:

  1. Praying (Solat)
  2. Speaking the truth
  3. Being kind to others
  4. Avoiding bad things

How to Obey Parents:

  1. Listening to them
  2. Greeting them
  3. Respecting and not arguing
