Security Precautions

Security precautions are the steps taken to make us free and safe from all dangers or arms.

Types of security Precaution

  1. Security precaution at home
  2. Security precaution at office
  3. Security precaution at school
  4. Personal security precautions

Sources of Danger

Sources of danger means what can cause danger to man.

Examples of sources of danger:

  1. Talking to strangers
  2. Fire out break
  3. Road accident
  4. Sharp objects eg Knife
  5. Wet floor
  6. Unsafe food

Security precaution on the road

  1. Know your safety road signal
  2. Obey traffic light
  3. Be careful while crossing the road
  4. Never run on busy road
  5. Look right and left before crossing the road
  6. Know and follow the road signs

Home security precaution

Home is a place we live

Security Precaution at home:

  1. Lock the doors and windows
  2. Keep the environment clean
  3. Always be watchful and careful
  4. Call for help when needed
  5. Turn off electrical appliances when not used
  6. Teach children on how to be safe at home

Security precaution at office

An office is a place of work

Security precaution at office:

  1. The office compound should be fenced and gated
  2. There should be a security at the office gate
  3. Staff should not share their identity card
  4. Clearance should be given to any visitors before entering the office premises
  5. Installation of CCTV camera for security purpose.

Personal security

Personal security is an extra caution taken to ensure your personal safety at all time.

Examples of personal security tips:

  1. Think fast and act smart
  2. Be vigilant
  3. Report strange and suspicious movement
  4. Avoid night walk or travelling
  5. Always tell your loved ones about your movement
  6. Be familiar with your environment and neighbours

Security precaution in the school

  1. Do not play with sharp objects
  2. Always think fast and act smart
  3. Report any form of bully
  4. Obey the school rules and regulation
  5. If you see something, say it

Precaution in the car/buses

  1. Do not sit in the front
  2. Always use seat belt
  3. Do not share seat belt
  4. Do not play in the car
  5. Do not stand while the bus is moving
  6. Do not wait at the door
